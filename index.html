Partfunds (Startup)

Partfunds is a startup which i was a part of with my fellow friends

It is a NFT marketplace, which is why we needed a few of our artworks and characters as a starter to put in the marketplace for onboarding the users


Designed a logo and planned the complete theme of the characters and vibe it is supposed to give, As the name suggests the ideology behind partfunds was to create a tradeplace where one could sell or buy the NFT part by part (like a piece). In order to represent this, i made the logo which shows the ideology of "part by part" concept, as you can see the the letter P is broken down to small parts which resembles the pieces of NFT that we are try to sell/buy.

My way of going about this was to make eye catchy characters and funky art as NFTs generic characteristic is to be unique, this gave me a big field to play with and experiment with unique ideas
